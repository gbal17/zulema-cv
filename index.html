About Me

PhD researcher in Nanosciences and Nanotechnology at NOVA School of Science and Technology in Lisbon. My work focuses on the surface characterization of nanostructured materials using quantitative X-ray photoelectron spectroscopy (XPS).
With a multidisciplinary background in membrane engineering and nanotechnology, I explore innovative materials for energy and sustainability applications. I enjoy working at the intersection of chemistry, physics, and materials science to solve complex challenges in nanomaterials research.
